# How to read this

- The two dashed lines are Danone's **$25** and **$27** all-cash bids. The gap between the price line and **$27** is the takeover discount the market is currently pricing.
- Vertical markers flag the control-defining dates: the **$27 bid**, Danone's **withdrawal**, the **June 17 annual meeting**, and the **poison-pill expiry**.
- A re-rate back toward the dashed lines is the strategic-re-bid scenario; a drift below is the market fading deal odds.

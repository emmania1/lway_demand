# Why milk matters

- Kefir's true milk input is **Class II** (soft/cultured products); Class III is the cheese grade. Class III is shown here as a closely-correlated, readily-available **proxy** until a live Class II series is wired.
- Milk is Lifeway's single largest input cost, so the **$/cwt line moving down is a direct gross-margin tailwind**.
- The retail CPI line (right axis) is the consumer-shelf proxy; a widening gap between cheap raw milk and firm retail pricing is the margin-expansion story playing out in the financials section.
